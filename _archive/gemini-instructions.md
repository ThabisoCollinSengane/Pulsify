## Pulsify — What Users Experience

### When you first open the site
- A dark themed app loads instantly with the **Pulsify logo** and a glowing orange dot
- A scrolling ticker at the top shows live event names across SA
- Top right shows a **Sign In** button

---

### Home Tab
- **Event cards** scroll horizontally — each showing event image, name, date, venue, price or FREE badge
- **Business cards** scroll below — shisanyamas, bars, clubs, hotels with ratings
- **Genre filter chips** — tap Gqom, Amapiano, Sport, Free etc to filter instantly
- Tap any event → full detail slides up with lineup, ticket tiers, buy button

---

### Discover Tab
- Search bar at the top — type any event, artist or venue name
- Results appear live as you type
- Filter by city, province and genre

---

### Map Tab
- Full screen live map of South Africa — blue ocean, lit roads
- Glowing coloured pins showing events and businesses across all 9 provinces
- Tap any pin → info panel slides up from bottom showing name, date, price and a button to view the full event or place
- Heatwave overlay shows hotspot areas
- Collapsible legend showing what each pin colour means

---

### Bookings Tab
- All your purchased tickets in one place
- Each booking shows event name, date, venue and a **QR code** for venue entry
- Booking reference number

---

### Friends Tab
- Search bar to find people by name or username
- Follow and unfollow with one tap
- See posts from people you follow
- Button to open the full social feed

---

### Profile Tab — logged out
- Sign In and Sign Up buttons

### Profile Tab — logged in
- Your avatar, display name and handle
- Stats — saved events, tickets bought, posts made
- Quick links to Feed, My Profile, My Bookings, Business Dashboard
- Sign Out button

---

### Social Feed (feeds.html)
- Posts from event organizers — announcements, promos, event hype
- Photo memories from users who attended events
- Like, comment and repost on every post
- Filter by All, Following, Organizers, Community
- Post button — organizers post freely, users must upload a photo from an event

---

### Sign Up flow
- Step 1 — name, username, email, password, city, province
- Step 2 — pick music genres, toggle organizer/business account, accept terms
- Creates a real account stored permanently in Supabase
- Redirected back to homepage logged in

---

### Business Portal (separate)
- Register your venue or event page with address — auto-placed on the map
- Dashboard showing revenue, bookings, followers, live orders
- Menu manager — add items with prices, photos and categories
- Customers can place pickup orders directly through the app
- Post updates that appear in your followers' feeds
- Create events that appear on the map and in the discover feed

---

### What makes it SA-specific
- All prices in **ZAR**
- Events and venues across all **9 provinces**
- SA genres — Gqom, Amapiano, Maskandi, Gospel, Shisanyama
- SA cities in all filters — Durban, Joburg, Cape Town, Umlazi, Margate etc
- Map centered on South Africa with ocean markers blocked

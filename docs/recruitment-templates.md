# ◉ PULSEFY — Recruitment Copy-Paste Templates

> Social media outreach templates for recruiting Event Organizers and Businesses to pulsefy.co.za

---

## How to Use

1. **Copy** the template text (everything below the banner line).
2. **Replace placeholders** before sending:
   - `[NAME]` → the person's first name (or "Hi there" if unknown)
   - `[EVENT NAME]` → the name of their event, festival, or night
   - `[BUSINESS NAME]` → the name of their venue, restaurant, or brand
   - `[YOUR NAME]` → your name (for sign-offs)
3. **Choose the right style** — DM, WhatsApp, or comment reply as labelled.
4. **Personalise one line** if you can (mention a recent event, a dish they're known for, etc.) — it massively improves response rates.
5. All templates link to **pulsefy.co.za** — the signup is free.

About Pulsify

Pulsify is South Africa's dedicated live event discovery platform — a Progressive Web App (no download required) built specifically for South Africans aged 18–35 who are looking for concerts, Amapiano nights, Gqom events, shisanyamas, festivals, and live experiences across KwaZulu-Natal, Gauteng, and Western Cape.

We combine a live event map, a home discovery feed, and a social layer — allowing users to invite friends, form squads, and check in to events they attend. Our genre filters include Gqom, Amapiano, Afrobeats, Jazz, Nightlife, and 15+ more categories.

The Opportunity

South Africa's live events market is one of the most culturally vibrant on the continent. Amapiano, Gqom, and Afrobeats have driven explosive growth in youth nightlife attendance over the last five years. Yet the discovery layer — how young South Africans find out where to go this weekend — remains fragmented across WhatsApp groups, Instagram stories, and word of mouth.

Pulsify is solving this. We are the single destination where South African youth discover, plan, and share live experiences. A sponsorship with Pulsify puts your brand at the exact moment of intent — when a 22-year-old in Durban is deciding where to go tonight.

There is a confirmed precedent for this partnership model in SA: Red Bull already uses Howler (a SA event-discovery app) as its exclusive ticketing and discovery partner for Red Bull Unlocked events. Amstel's Friends of Amstel events are the most prominent Amapiano concert platform in the country. These brands have proven that discovery + beverage = powerful brand activation.

What a Partnership Looks Like

Example: Amstel × Pulsify "Friends of Amstel" Edition

  • Every Friends of Amstel event listed on Pulsify with Amstel branding embedded in the event page
  • The Amapiano genre filter is renamed "🎶 Friends of Amstel" during the event campaign period
  • Push notification sent to all Durban + Joburg + Cape Town users: "Friends of Amstel is coming to your city — tap to discover"
  • Pulsify social posts (cross-posted to Amstel's channels) in the lead-up to events
  • Post-event: aggregate check-in data and demographic breakdown delivered to Amstel's marketing team

Example: Red Bull × Pulsify Durban Expansion

  • Red Bull Unlocked Durban listed exclusively on Pulsify, mirroring the Howler model in Cape Town/Joburg
  • Red Bull branded map pin — all Red Bull events appear with a custom pin on the Pulsify live map
  • Red Bull Basement 2027 competition promoted to Pulsify's tech-curious 18–35 user base
  • Red Bull Studio Cape Town content distributed through the Pulsify community feed

Why Now

  • Amapiano and Gqom are at peak cultural relevance: SA-born genres are charting globally. Brands that align now capture the wave.
  • Discovery is the gap: Event organisers post on Instagram and hope for the best. Pulsify gives brands a structured, measurable discovery channel with audience data.
  • PWA = frictionless reach: No app store download means Pulsify can reach any SA smartphone user via a link or QR code. Your campaign activation distributes itself.
  • KZN is underserved: Most event-tech platforms are Cape Town and Joburg-centric. Pulsify has deep roots in Durban and KZN — a market with massive appetite and limited brand activation infrastructure.
  • 3rd-party events already live: Ticketmaster SA events are already displaying on Pulsify with a "Buy on Ticketmaster" flow. The platform is production-ready for brand partner event integration.

What is Pulsify?

Pulsify is a South African platform that helps people aged 18–35 discover where to go — shisanyamas, clubs, restaurants, live music, events, and places to stay overnight. We have users across KwaZulu-Natal, Gauteng, and Western Cape, all actively looking for the next great experience.

We built Pulsify because places like Eyadini, Joe Cools, KwaMaxs, and Egagasini are packed on weekends — but thousands of potential customers nearby still don't know they exist, or don't know what's happening there this weekend. We fix that.

No website? No problem. Your Pulsify listing is your online presence.

The Problem We're Solving For You

  • You post on Instagram or WhatsApp, but only your existing followers see it
  • People nearby have no easy way to discover you
  • You don't have a website, or maintaining one is expensive and time-consuming
  • Competitors with more social media budget get found first on Google

Pulsify puts you on a live map and in a discovery feed — exactly where people are already searching.

How It Works

1

Your venue appears on the Pulsify live map

Users open the map on their phone and see your pin near them. One tap opens your full profile: photos, description, menu/vibe, events this weekend, contact details, and a location link.

2

You post your specials, events, and updates

Through your free business dashboard, you can post what's happening — a Friday night special, a live DJ, a weekend buffet, a public holiday event — in minutes. It goes live on the feed and map immediately.

3

Pulsify users discover and share you

Users follow your venue, share your events with friends, form squads to come through, and check in when they arrive. Every share is free advertising for you.

4

Customers walk through your door

More people know about you. More people come. Simple.

Common Questions

Do I need a website or social media page?

No. Your Pulsify profile replaces a website. If you already have Instagram or Facebook, we can link them — but it's not required.

How long does it take to set up?

About 5 minutes for a basic listing. We also offer a free white-glove setup — call or WhatsApp us and we'll create your listing for you.

Do I need a smartphone or laptop?

You can manage your listing from any smartphone browser. No app download required.

What if I don't have event photos?

We can use photos from your social media pages (with your permission), or list you with a placeholder image while you gather photos. Don't let missing photos stop you — get listed first.

Is Pulsify only for Durban?

Pulsify covers KwaZulu-Natal, Gauteng, and Western Cape. Durban is our home base, but we're expanding nationally.

Can I list my BnB or guesthouse?

Yes — we have a dedicated accommodation category. Travellers and weekend visitors actively use Pulsify to find places to stay near the events they're attending.
